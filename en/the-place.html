A timeless bistro in the heart of Paris

Le Florentin is one of those rare Parisian bistros where the decor hasn't been designed — it has been lived in. The delicate mouldings, mirrors that have reflected a century of conversations, hand-carved woodwork: everything here is the real thing.

The deep red that dresses the dining room isn't an interior designer's choice. It's the colour of the great Parisian bistros — the kind that warms a room and gives faces that particular glow under the lamplight.

Whether you come for lunch between Place de la Concorde and the Tuileries Garden, or for dinner before an evening on the Champs-Élysées, Le Florentin is the kind of place that turns a meal into a memory.
