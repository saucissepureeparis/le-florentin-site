A bistro with a soul

Steps from Place de la Concorde, Le Florentin carries on the art of the Parisian bistro. Period mouldings, antique mirrors, woodwork softened by time — every detail tells a century of Parisian life.

The kitchen serves the dishes that made France famous: Burgundy escargots, French onion soup gratinée, duck confit. Classics prepared with fresh ingredients, served with the generosity of a true bistro.
